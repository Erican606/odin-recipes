Peanut Butta Jelly

The best thing to happen to the world since sliced bread.

The Peanut Butta and Jelly sammich.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butta (Creamy no nuts or else)
  • Jelly (Except it has to be Jam/and Grape flavoured)
  • Bread (only allowed to use Hawaiian Sweet bread)

Preperation

  1. Take you're new load of Hawaiian Sweet bread that I made you buy for this recipe and throw it in the fridge.

Steps

  1. Remove the two middle slices of bread from you're nice cold bread
  2. With a butter knife of you're choice, slather one piece of bread with peanut butter.
  3. Now with a new clean butter knife, slather the other piece with jelly. (Jam)
  4. (Make sure to have a nice even spread.)
  5. Slap one piece of you're nicely spread peanut butter or jelly (JAM) onto the other.

And presto. The perfect Peanut Butta and Jelly
